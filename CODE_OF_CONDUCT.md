# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We want Super Bash Folds to be a welcoming project for players, artists,
animators, designers, developers, and first-time open-source contributors.
Participation must remain respectful, constructive, and safe regardless of
experience, background, identity, or ability.

## Expected behavior

- Give feedback about the work, not the person.
- Be specific, patient, and open to correction.
- Respect licenses, attribution, privacy, and contributor boundaries.
- Make room for newcomers and explain project conventions when they are not
  obvious.
- Accept a maintainer decision to pause or redirect work when legal, safety, or
  project-scope concerns are unresolved.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized attention, personal attacks,
doxxing, spam, deliberate disruption, and knowingly submitting unlicensed or
stolen content are not permitted.

## Enforcement

Report conduct problems privately to the repository owner through the contact
method on their GitHub profile. Include links or screenshots when possible.
Maintainers may edit or remove contributions, restrict participation, or ban a
participant when necessary to protect the community.

This policy applies in repository spaces and when someone represents the
project in public community spaces.
