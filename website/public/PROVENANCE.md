# Website launch-media provenance

These files are distributed by the public website. Their exact hashes are in
[`SHA256SUMS`](SHA256SUMS). The finished project-created files listed here are
released under [CC0 1.0](https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/).

## Brand icons

- `favicon.svg` is an original project mark created with GPT-5.6-Sol under
  Swarek's direction and review. It is an exact copy of `public/favicon.svg`.
- `icon.png` is a 512 x 512 PNG rasterization of that SVG, produced with macOS
  `sips` for browser and installed-app metadata.

## Social artwork

- `og.png` was generated with OpenAI image generation under Swarek's direction
  and review, using the credited CC0 Quaternius hero, Quaternius cactus, and
  Verdant Grove project art as visual sources. It was framed at 1200 x 630 for
  link previews.
- `github-social.jpg` is a JPEG transcode of `og.png`, produced with FFmpeg to
  satisfy GitHub's social-preview upload limit.

The underlying source packs and their authors are documented in
[`THIRD_PARTY_ASSETS.md`](https://github.com/Swarek/Super_Bash_Folds/blob/main/THIRD_PARTY_ASSETS.md).

## Public gameplay media

- Source author and recorder: Swarek.
- Source: an original 2026-07-16 capture of the redistributable public build.
  The raw recording is not distributed; these derivatives contain no
  third-party franchise footage.
- `media/gameplay.webm`: cropped and transcoded public gameplay loop, edited
  with GPT-5.6-Sol under Swarek's direction and review.
- `media/gameplay-poster.webp`: still frame exported from the same public loop.
